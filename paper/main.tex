\begin{abstract}
This paper documents the Rosenbrock differential-algebraic equation (DAE) solver implementation in MICM, with a focus on architecture, numerical properties, and benchmark behavior for stiff atmospheric chemistry systems. This scaffold is intentionally lightweight and is intended to be expanded section-by-section with validated results and reproducibility artifacts.
